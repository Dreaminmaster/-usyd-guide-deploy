# Source policy

## Authority order

1. The student's own Offer, CoE, visa grant letter, University email and authenticated system record.
2. University of Sydney and Centre for English Teaching official pages.
3. Relevant Australian or NSW government authority pages.
4. University of Sydney Union official pages for USU services.
5. Secondary explanations only when a first-party source does not answer the question; such content must be clearly identified and is not used for binding rules.

## Writing rules

- Do not convert a common experience into a University rule.
- Do not state a deadline, fee, visa condition or eligibility rule without an authoritative source.
- Preserve important qualifications, exceptions, session-specific dates and processing times.
- For operational guides, include prerequisites, exact navigation path, numbered steps, expected wait, failure symptoms, troubleshooting and original links.
- When an official page conflicts with personal documents, the personal document or authenticated system record wins; the student should contact the responsible office.
- Checked dates indicate when the source was reviewed, not a guarantee that a policy will never change.
